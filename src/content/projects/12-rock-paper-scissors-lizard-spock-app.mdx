<blockquote>
  Scissors cuts paper, paper covers rock, rock crushes lizard, lizard poisons Spock, Spock smashes scissors, scissors
  decapitates lizard, lizard eats paper, paper disproves Spock, Spock vaporizes rock, and as it always has, rock crushes
  scissors.
</blockquote>

This is a solution to the Rock, paper, scissors, lizard, spock challenge on [Frontend Mentor!](https://www.frontendmentor.io/challenges/rock-paper-scissors-game-pTgwgvgH)

## Overview

Users should be able to:

- View the optimal layout for the game depending on their device's screen size
- Play Rock, Paper, Scissors against the computer
- Maintain the state of the score after refreshing the browser (optional)
- **Bonus**: Play Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock against the computer (optional)
