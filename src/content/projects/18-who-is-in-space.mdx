The International Space Station (ISS) and who is currently up there. The station's mission is to serve as a unique microgravity laboratory for scientific research, enabling long-term human space exploration and providing benefits to Earth.

This project is built using React, TypeScript, TanStack Router, TanStack Query and Tailwind CSS. It uses differtent APIs to pull various information.

<Figure image={FirstImage} alt="Front Page" caption="Front Page" />

## Features

- ISS Tracker powered by the [WTIA API](https://wheretheiss.at/w/developer) and [Leaflet](https://leafletjs.com/)
- Astronaut Information provied by [The Space Devs](https://thespacedevs.com/) and their [Launch Library 2 API](https://thespacedevs.com/llapi) and the [Wiklpedia API](https://www.wikipedia.org/) for more details about the astronauts
- NASA News Feed provied by [The Space Devs](https://thespacedevs.com/) and their [Spaceflight News API](https://thespacedevs.com/snapi)
